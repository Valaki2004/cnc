Brotherhood of nod


The Brotherhood of Nod (often shortened to Nod or the Brotherhood) is a worldwide, quasi-religious and political movement devoted to the charismatic figure of Kane, and the harnessing of the extraterrestrial Tiberium substance by Earth's downtrodden peoples. Although the Brotherhood first emerged in 1995, the year of Tiberium's arrival, Nod had existed as an ancient and secret society long before this, and claims to have originally been founded around 1800 BC. During the course of the 21st century, the Brotherhood began to exhibit the combined characteristics of a world religion, a massive decentralized nation-state, a high-tech multinational corporation, and an unorthodox superpower. Core Nod beliefs include that Kane is an eternal prophet figure, whose guidance to humanity allegedly dates back through millennia of secrecy, and that Tiberium represents the catalyst for the next stage of human evolution. At the end of the 20th century, the Brotherhood was officially designated a worldwide terrorist organization by the United Nations-sanctioned Global Defense Initiative; for dangerous misuses of the Tiberium substance, the founding of a rogue military-industrial complex throughout disenfranchised countries, instigating social and political upheaval across first world nations, and for pursuing a total domination over the world's peoples and resources.The Brotherhood would consequently face off against the UN's GDI in four successive world wars, restoring itself after two military defeats, and finally achieving Kane's vision during the later two conflicts, bringing Nod's prophecy to its fulfillment
